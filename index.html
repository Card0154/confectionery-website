History of Coco's Chocolatiers

Run by the Deschamps family, Coco's Chocolatiers has been an active part of the Belleville community since the 1950s. Located in downtown Belleville, the quaint shop occupies a populated street corner and is a must-see for those wanting the comforts of home. The shop is considered a landmark to local sweet-enthusiasts. One of the most memorable aspects of the shop is the decor; an homage to the classic look of the shops during the early 1900s.
